Welcome to NEW MARUTI TRADERS

General Merchant & Commision Agent

Serving Since 2000 | Trusted FMCG Distribution Partner

Established in the year 2000, NEW MARUTI TRADERS is a leading FMCG distribution and wholesale supply business based in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh. We specialize in delivering high-quality fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) to retailers, wholesalers, hotels, supermarkets, and institutional buyers with reliability, speed, and trust.

With years of experience and strong logistics support, we ensure timely delivery, competitive pricing, and genuine branded products. Our goal is to empower retailers by providing a dependable supply chain and excellent service.
